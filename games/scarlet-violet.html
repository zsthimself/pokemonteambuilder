Scarlet & Violet Metagame Analysis

The Scarlet & Violet metagame introduces Terastallization, allowing Pokemon to change their type during battle. This mechanic adds a new layer of strategy to team building, as you can cover weaknesses or enhance strengths with the right Tera Type.

Key Pokemon to consider for your Scarlet & Violet team:

  • Gholdengo - Excellent typing and stats make it a formidable special attacker
  • Iron Treads - Great defensive typing and access to Rapid Spin
  • Dragonite - Versatile attacker with access to Multiscale and powerful moves
  • Kingambit - Powerful physical attacker with unique ability
  • Great Tusk - Excellent bulk and offensive presence
