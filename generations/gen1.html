Generation I Metagame Analysis

The Generation I metagame is dominated by Psychic-type Pokemon due to their lack of effective counters. Only Ghost and Bug types are supposed to be super effective against Psychic, but due to a programming error, Ghost-type moves have no effect on Psychic types. Additionally, the only strong Bug-type move is Twineedle, which isn't very powerful.

Key Pokemon to consider for your Generation I team:

  • Alakazam - Incredible Special stat and Speed make it a top-tier threat
  • Chansey - Enormous HP and Special make it nearly impossible to take down
  • Exeggutor - Great Special stat and access to Sleep Powder and Explosion
  • Tauros - Excellent all-around stats and a powerful Normal-type STAB
  • Snorlax - Huge HP, good Special, and access to Rest and Body Slam
